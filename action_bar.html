Socialize Action Bar¶

Introduction¶

v0.5.0 of the Socialize SDK introduced the “Action Bar” which provides a single entry point into the entire set of Socialize features.

Displaying the Action Bar¶

Using the SocializeActionBar is very simple. Instantiate a SocializeActionBar controller and add the view to your view controller:

By default, the Action Bar will automatically place itself at the bottom of its superview and adjust to rotation. If you find that content is being hidden, one option is to ensure that 44 pixels are left empty at the bottom of your view. When using interface builder, this is as simple as sliding up the bottom of any content.
